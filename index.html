This is The Ping Pong Game

The Rules are simple!

PING! is worth 5 points. PONG! is worth 7 points. PING-PONG! is worth 10 points.

The player with the most points wins!

Enter a number that is divisible by 3 with no remainder and get a PING!

Enter a number that is divisible by 5 with no remainder and get a PONG!

Enter a number that is divisible by 3 & 5 wit no remainder and get a PING-PONG!
